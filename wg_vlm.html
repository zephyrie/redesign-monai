Vision Language Models
Working Group

Mission Statement

The MONAI VLM working group aims to advance the integration of multi-modal capabilities, including LLMs and VLMs, within the MONAI ecosystem. We focus on establishing best practices for building, evaluating, and deploying VLM clinical models while facilitating research and development in medical imaging applications.
